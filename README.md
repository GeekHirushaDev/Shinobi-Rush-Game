        <h2>Overview</h2>
        <p>Shinobi Rush is a fast-paced 2D platformer developed using HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. In this game, players
            take on the role of a skilled ninja traversing through a series of challenging levels filled with traps,
            obstacles, and enemies. The objective is to reach the end of each level while avoiding hazards and
            collecting power-ups along the way.</p>

        <h2>Features</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><b>Dynamic Obstacle Course:</b> Navigate through a variety of obstacles such as spikes, pits, moving
                platforms, and enemy ninjas.</li>
            <li><b>Power-Ups:</b> Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities, such as speed boosts, invincibility
                shields, and double jumps.</li>
            <li><b>Multiple Levels:</b> Explore different themed levels, each with its own unique challenges and
                obstacles to overcome.</li>
            <li><b>High Score System:</b> Compete with friends and other players for the highest score on each level
                and aim to become the ultimate Shinobi Rush master.</li>
            <li><b>Responsive Controls:</b> Enjoy smooth and responsive controls, allowing for precise movements and
                jumps to tackle even the toughest obstacles.</li>
        </ul>

        <h2>How to Play</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><b>Space Key:</b> Use the space key to execute ninja jumps.</li>
            <li><b>Enter Key:</b> Press Enter to start the game.</li>
        </ul>

        <h2>Tips for Success</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><b>Pay Attention to Character Speed:</b> Your ninja's pace may increase, requiring quick adjustments to
                dodge obstacles effectively.</li>
            <li><b>Master Jump Mechanics:</b> Experiment with jump timings to optimize your leaps over obstacles.</li>
            <li><b>Plan Ahead:</b> Scan ahead to anticipate upcoming obstacles and plan your moves accordingly.</li>
            <li><b>Hone Your Timing:</b> Practice your jumps to enhance timing and accuracy, crucial for conquering
                challenging obstacles.</li>
            <li><b>Stay Patient and Persistent:</b> Success in Shinobi Rush requires perseverance. Don't be disheartened
                by initial failures; keep practicing and refining your skills to overcome obstacles and progress
                through the game.</li>
        </ul>
